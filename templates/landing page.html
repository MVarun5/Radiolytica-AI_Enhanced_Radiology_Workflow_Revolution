About Radiolytica

Radiolytica is an AI-powered radiology assistant that detects critical diseases from chest X-rays within seconds. Our deep learning models analyze pneumonia, tuberculosis, and cardiomegaly with high accuracy, helping radiologists and medical professionals make faster, more reliable diagnoses.

With a focus on efficiency and precision, Radiolytica revolutionizes the radiology workflow, ensuring early disease detection and improved patient outcomes.
